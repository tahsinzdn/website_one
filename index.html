Introduction to Batting

Batting in cricket refers to the act of using a bat to hit the ball. It's one of the fundamental skills required in the game. A good batsman can score runs efficiently while avoiding getting out.

Batting Techniques

There are several techniques in batting that players should master, including:

  • Grip: Hold the bat with your dominant hand on top.
  • Stance: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent.
  • Backlift: Raise the bat behind you in preparation to strike the ball.
  • Footwork: Move your feet towards the ball to get into the correct position.

Batting Tips

Here are some tips to improve your batting skills:

  • Keep your eye on the ball at all times.
  • Practice different shots like drives, cuts, and pulls.
  • Focus on timing rather than just power.
  • Stay calm and composed under pressure.
